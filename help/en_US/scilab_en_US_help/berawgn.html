BERAWGN

Bit error rate (BER) for uncoded AWGN channels

Calling Sequence

ber = berawgn(Ebno, scheme) 
Returns the BER of uncoded BPSK over an AWGN channel with coherent demodulation.

Arguments

  • EbNo - The ratio of bit energy to noise power spectral density, in dB. If EbNo is a vector, the output ber is a vector of the same size, whose elements correspond to the different Eb/N0 levels.
  • Scheme - Modulation scheme

Description

The berawgn function returns the BER of various modulation schemes over an additive white Gaussian noise (AWGN) channel

Examples

berawgn([3 1.6],'bpsk')
ber = [0.0071529    0.0368191]
